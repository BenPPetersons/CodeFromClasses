Policy

At Skydrive Innovations, we are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard your information.

1. Information We Collect

We collect personal information you provide directly to us, such as when you register for an account, subscribe to our newsletter, or purchase a product. The types of information we collect may include your name, email address, phone number, and payment information.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to operate, maintain, and improve our services. This may include processing transactions, providing customer support, sending updates, and personalizing content.

3. Sharing of Information

We do not share or sell your personal information to third parties without your consent, except to comply with legal obligations, protect our rights, or fulfill a business transaction.

4. Data Security

We implement security measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

No online data may be entirely secure, so please take caution with any online activity, including sending online data to Skydrive Innovations.

5. Your Choices

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal information. You may also opt out of certain data collection practices by adjusting your browser settings or unsubscribing from marketing communications.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy periodically. If significant changes are made, we will notify you via email or through our website.

Contact us at support@skdriveinovations.com if you have any questions regarding this policy.
